Design Direction: "Wabi-Sabi Editorial"

  • Japanese Indigo primary (#3d5a80) - Aizome, traditional Japanese dye color
  • Vermillion secondary (#c73e3a) - Shu-iro, used for emphasis and favorites
  • Cool white backgrounds (#ffffff, #f8f9fa) - not warm cream
  • High contrast ink black text (#1a1d21) - clear, readable
  • Sharp corners (2-4px) - intentional, not bubbly
  • No hover lift effects - subtle border interactions only
  • No left-border decorations on cards - clean edges
  • Source Sans 3 for UI, Noto Sans JP for Japanese
  • 8pt spacing grid for consistent rhythm
  • Accessibility: skip links, focus-visible, reduced motion
