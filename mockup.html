Design Direction: "Ghibli Garden"

  • Sage green primary (#5B8A72) - forest/nature inspired, calming yet focused
  • Soft sky blue secondary (#7BA4C4) - like Ghibli's painted skies
  • Warm clay pink tertiary (#D4A5A5) - sunset warmth, gentle accent
  • Warm white backgrounds (#FAFBF9) - easy on eyes for long reading
  • Charcoal text (#2D3A2E) - softer than pure black, less eye strain
  • Slightly rounded corners (6px) - friendly but not bubbly
  • Clean underline tabs - focused, not distracting
  • No warm terracotta/coral - avoids AI slop aesthetic
  • No cream backgrounds - fresher, more natural feel
  • Promotes calm productivity - not sleepy, not anxious
